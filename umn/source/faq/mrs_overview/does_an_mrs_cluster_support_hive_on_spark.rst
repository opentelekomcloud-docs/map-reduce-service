Does an MRS Cluster Support Hive on Spark?
==========================================

-  Clusters of MRS 1.9.\ *x* support Hive on Spark.
-  Clusters of MRS 3.\ *x* or later support Hive on Spark.
-  You can use Hive on Tez for the clusters of other versions.
